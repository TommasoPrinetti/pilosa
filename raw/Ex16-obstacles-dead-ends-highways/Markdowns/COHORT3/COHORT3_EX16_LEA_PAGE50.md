# Ex16b - Dead Ends

**Instructions**

Dead Ends are moments when you feel truly stuck, unable to progress, or at a loss as to what to do next.

1. Begin by describing the earliest signs that led you to conclude that you had hit a dead end.
    - Did the LLM keep looping or repeating itself?
    - Did it contradict itself or misunderstand your input again and again?

2. Then, focus on the specific approaches you tried before concluding it was truly a dead end.
    - Did you insist on a solution?
    - Did you explore other strategies?

3. Reflect on what made this moment different from a typical obstacle:
    - what clues or patterns told you it was time to stop?

4. Finally, consider the emotional and intellectual toll of encountering a dead end.
    - Were you frustrated, resigned, exhausted?
