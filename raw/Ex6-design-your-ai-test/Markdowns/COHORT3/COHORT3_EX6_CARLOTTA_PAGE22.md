## Task Fit
In what way does this task reflect a core part of your job or expertise?
Is it something you do regularly or only on occasion? Is it a boring or an exciting task?

Pitching is something we do almost on a daily basis, or regularly.
After we found a new story idea we need to sell it to make it exist -> to a media or to our boss.
It's quite annoying as we really need to convince and persuade as if we were vendors.

## Professional Relevance
Does the task highlight any specific skills or crucial professional expertise? Are there any industry norms, professional codes, or expectations that this task should respect?

There are many industry norms ->
- pitch need to be suited for our target media, match their line.
- angle need to be fresh / new
- show we already conducted research - figures, some facts to show topic relevance
- answer questions -> why audience / editors should care? why now? how?

## Personal Outcome
How will evaluating the LLM on this task guide you toward the model that suits your work best?

- Ability of LLM to apply all guidelines
- Need to be catchy, concise, precise.
- Ability of LLM to conduct benchmark to find best suited media for my story.
- Efficient phrasing.
