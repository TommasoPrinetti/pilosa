## Rating the result
**Does this task need to be perfect, or is there room for "good enough"?**
**How will you decide if the LLM's performance is sufficient to support you professionally?**

### Failure
**What would be a dealbreaker in your field?**
Usually if we do not provide the "arrêt", it is likely to give you a summary about a case that has nothing to do with what you're working on + fails if forgets important details such as what was the q° asked to the Court & sol° given

### Good enough
**What would be a usable or helpful result?**
If explains the facts leading to the q° & gives the solution brought by the Court

### Success
**What would be a perfect result?**
Perfect if able to sum up the "arrêt" and don't miss important details in the facts that makes the decision applicable only in similar situations.
