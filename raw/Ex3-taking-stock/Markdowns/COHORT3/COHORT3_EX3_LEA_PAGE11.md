# Ex3c - The Story Told by Your LLM

*If someone were to look solely at your LLM conversations to understand your work, what aspects would be overlooked or misrepresented?*

Reflect on the shadows, missing context, and the work that happens before
and after using the LLM, which these conversations might not capture.

Most of the time, the question is uncorrelated with previous contents,
or vague enough not to let the model know about how its answer
will be used (if ever used) and under what specific circumstances -
despite giving a backdrop to the prompt/question is necessary for
relevant answers.

- the important factor is striking a balance to provide relevant
enough context for accurate answers without -
jeopardizing the work or data you have been entrusted
with.

Further, it doesn't capture the discussions and modifications
made on projects based on teamwork which we often rely
on in our working process
