# Ex3c - The Story Told by Your LLM

*What have you done for the machine?*
Reflect on the work you've provided and the time you've invested to get good results out of the LLM.

My share of time provided to LLMs remains negligible compared to
browsing and library-based research. However, the progression I have
is exponential as I get familiarised with the ways in which
I could rely more on LLMs - especially enclosing
documents for reviews or else.

I remain mindful & vigilant with the data provided however.
I believe that I ~~use~~ not to resort to LLMs much because
I felt like the time needed to get good results was longer than
if I were to search online by myself.

This may be because of the interface & the layout of answers
given in paragraphs - a format I am not so used to when
reading articles where you can get to the conclusion or the
intro in different manners as they clearly stand out.
