# Ex3c - The Story Told by Your LLM

*What has the machine done for you?*
Describe how the LLM has been helpful and valuable to your work.

LLMs have helped me breakdown parts of my projects and
work-related contents I needed to analyse or reflect on.
It is efficient in highlighting key terms of debates as well as
making sure that we completely understand certain
concepts and ideas.
Usually however, when correlating ideas - even if the tie is
thin - it follows the <u>request</u> and does not object much.
In this sense, its deterministic design is not rooted in an
end-user tailored engineering approach as it aims at being
"general"; a feature that inherently establishes its limits.
Hence, the machine has been helpful in answering general
questions and pedagogical request while discipline-focused
matters ~~were~~ less frequently met with successful output.
