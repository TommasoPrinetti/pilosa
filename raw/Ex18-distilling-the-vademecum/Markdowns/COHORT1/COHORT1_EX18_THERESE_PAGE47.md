# Ex18 - Mémoire : différenciation fonctionnelle de la société moderne

février 2025

I used Chatgpt to deep into a complex notion of sociology for my master thesis. I used it to find definitions, to cite precisions and to confront my ideas on it, because the notion of sociology was quite confuse.

-it works very well to find definitions and compare them accross authors of a same field
-it works well to synethize my problem and offer paths of solutions.
-it works well only if you know enough of the subject to go deeper, because #I knew already some paradoxes of the notion. But Chatgpt otherwise stops its explanation before the obstacle. Without the previous knowledge, you stay at a superficial level.
- a bit unstable : it's efficient, but ya know you have to check everything afterwards.
