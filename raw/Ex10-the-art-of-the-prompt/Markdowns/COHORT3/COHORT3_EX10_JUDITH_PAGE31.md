# Ex10b - Learning Your Craft

Keep Track of Tips and Tricks

* fact check for hallucinations

* Tip: keep my prompt library.

Tricks
* prioritise instructions e.g. act as a senior partner.
* be detailed and avoid your biases e.g. use service person/worker (non-gendered terms)
* give the LLM as much detail and data to work with
* give it your preferences.
* explaining ur style to the LLM: be clear e.g. use 2-3 sentences to explain the concept of prompt engineering to a high school student.
* avoid saying what not to do but say what to do. use words like refrain from... if you don't have... (recommend instead).
