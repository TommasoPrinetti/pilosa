# Ex5c - Confronting perspectives

## Instructions

You have now built your lexicon of terms through a series of subtractions. What do you think of the final terms?
Do they adequately describe who is writing/speaking?
Do some feel uncanny or weird to use? Why?

The final terms are the most « original » ones, « out of the box », in the sense that they differ from what we generally come up when thinking about LLM's use.
They may feel weird to use because they depict persons with very specific functions not generally attributed to a machine (ex HR manager).

Think back to your fellow co-inquirers' responses. What stood out to you? Did they use terms that you would never use? Did you have some in common?

What stood out from all the terms is the idea that we mainly think about LLMs / AI machine as being able to assist us and provide us knowledge
So the words most commons were : assistant, teacher, students etc... .
