# Ex7b - Choosing your core four

## Instructions

You have 10-15 minutes to select four tasks according to the following grid.

Remember: you will rely on these four tasks to choose a model for the rest of the experiments!

Choose tasks that **matter to you** professionally. These tasks should be a **core part of your professional practices**, whether they are mundane or extraordinary, frequent or rare, whether you would actually delegate them or hesitate to. You can **include a task** even if you think an LLM would perform poorly, because failing this task would be a meaningful result in itself.

## The core-grid:

| | Okay to delegate | Hesitant to delegate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Have to** | dist cases articles about a specific subject and extract main info | List and summarizing judicial decisions related to a specific legal notion / case<br>④ summarizing a law court |
| **Like to** | Write counter legal arguments (ex for a procedure). | Solve a practical case (legal case). |
