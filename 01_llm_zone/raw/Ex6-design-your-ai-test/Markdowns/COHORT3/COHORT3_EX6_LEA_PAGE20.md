### Failure
**What would be a deal-breaker in your field?**
Repetition or agglomeration of fragments of previous similar events in history that may not be coherent with the cultural and societal context of / the organisation / at hand in the analysis / the country

### Good Enough
**What would be a usable or helpful result?**
Informed and explainable chain of events with coherent and realistic moves.
Possibility to explain the scenario to a laymen audience and potential to be replicated.

### Success
**What would be a perfect result?**
Consistent scenario that displays in-depth understanding of each player's interests and objectives (Bayes Nets + Prisoner Dilemma).
Detailed main events / main incidents list that is consistent throughout the scenario.
