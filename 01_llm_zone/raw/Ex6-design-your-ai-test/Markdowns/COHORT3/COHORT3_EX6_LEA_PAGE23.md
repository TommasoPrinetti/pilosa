## Rating the Result

Does this task need to be perfect, or is there room for "good enough"?
How will you decide if the LLM's performance is sufficient to support you professionally?

### Failure
What would be a deal-breaker in your field?

Hallucinations, false information or unreliable sources used to justify certain risks or add opinionated detail to the news report. A response that is limited to descriptive analysis.

### Good Enough
What would be a usable or helpful result?

Nuanced and cautious tone on a gathering of pieces of information from international and local sources which can be checked for validity (traceability). Drafting one or two recommendations based on the audience the report is meant to be written to.

### Success
What would be a perfect result?

Sourced result with local and international coverage distinguished and compared, with historical contextualisation to assess the significance of the event from which recommendations on the way forward can be drawn.
