Language: a bit verbose and redundant,

-> First iteration: reduce the jargon and keep it at email length -> the summary became too brief. Client couldn't find it natural.

-> By the third trial, could still not appeal to the client. Had to re-prompt and mention that the email needs to be warm and encourage the client to trust us again.

-> For all the trials, content wise it was accurate -> in terms of the facts but a little giving it away. On prompting on this problem, the chatbot corrected the issue and enhanced it in such a manner that it would render the client wanting more.
