# Ex6 - Top & Flop [Asynchronous]

**Instructions**
Go through your history of conversations (Ex 02).

Select 3-5 conversations in which the LLM performed well. Select another 3-5 conversations in which the LLM performed poorly. Some conversations can be similar to your memorable conversations (Ex 03a).

**Assign them letters** (F to Z). Say, in a few words, why the LLM did either a good or a bad job.

Keep thinking about Ex04 throughout the week ("Finding tasks for the machine").

**Deadline**
This exercise is due for October 14th. you're asked to bring it completed.
