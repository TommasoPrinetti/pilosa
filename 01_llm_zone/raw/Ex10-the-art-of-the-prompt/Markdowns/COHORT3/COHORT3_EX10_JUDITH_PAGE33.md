# Ex10c - Practice Makes Perfect

What specific adjustments did you make?
*Constraints : I replaced "provide all possible solutions" with "draft a client-ready memorandum" which elevated the quality from the initial all-over-the place draft to a professional one.
max 3 pages : the cap forced the LLM to prioritize and be concise. It eliminated the general analysis and verbosity
*context refinement : act as a senior partner in a corporate law firm. (I also copied 3 paras of a formerly done memo -> the LLM kind of adopted the formality and authoritative language of a top tier legal advisor.
*creativity and strategic scope : "act as a highly innovative lawyer" or include a "non-standard acquisition strategy" :
With this prompt I saw that the LLM's tried to give me some interesting options alongside the usual predictable solutions.
* "use specific contractual terms of a JV"
