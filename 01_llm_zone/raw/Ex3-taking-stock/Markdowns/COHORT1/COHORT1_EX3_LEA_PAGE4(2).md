# Ex2 - Taking stock / Activity

1) I would delegate these tasks to AI without thinking twice: translation, summary, basic research I can do on google, leisure related (travel advice, hiking plans, sport / running plan etc...), calculation.

2) I would be hesitant to delegate: legal tasks (such as : summarize a judicial decision, write a contract, answer a legal question, design a litigation strategy, explain a legal notion); health related questions; personal question (how privacy is ensured); write a political opinion paper (ex. "How AI should be regulated by the EU"); economic question (ex how to solve public debt ?).
NB summarization = interpretation

3) Tasks I would never delegate: emotion related (breakup text ?) and personal (ex present me for a motivation letter); deep thinking production (research paper for example); predict a judicial decision (how much reputation? prob of winning ?)
