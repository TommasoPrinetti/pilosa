My prompting:

Having gone through the prompting rules when using LLMs, I would have been more strategic while phrasing to ensure the LLM is able to execute everything to the best of its ability.

For my subsequent prompts, I will follow the guideline I have obtained from the materials we have been provided, being:
* Instruction: specifying the task/instruction that I want the LLM to execute.
* Context: additional information that can help the LLM to produce a better outcome.
* Input data: the question that I need a response for
* Output indicator: the type/format of the output.
