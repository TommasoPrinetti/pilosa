## Obstacles
Obstacles are moments when you encounter problems you ultimately overcame or believe you can still overcome.

Begin by describing simple things, giving specific examples:
> How do you recognize you're in such a situation?
> How much of a setback was it?
> What strategies did you try to get out of this obstacle?
> What finally enables you to get unstuck?
> Why do you think it worked vs the previous things you tried?

You can then reflect on the broader issues of these obstacles.
