# Ex4 - Finding tasks for the machine [Asynchronous]

Instructions:
During the week, keep in mind tasks that could be automated by the machine. Think about it in class; when you speak with friends, teachers, mentors; etc. Take some notes and bring your ideas to our next session.

Deadline
This exercise will stretch for the whole experiment length. You're asked to complete it throughout it.

et ses nouvelles politiques publiques
