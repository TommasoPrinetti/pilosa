# Ex4 - Finding tasks for the machine [Asynchronous]

**Instructions**
During the week, keep in mind tasks that could be automated by the machine. Think about it in class; when you speak with friends, teachers, mentors; etc. Take some notes and bring your ideas to our next session.

**Deadline**
This exercise will stretch for the whole experiment length. You're asked to complete it throughout it.

Notes I directly asked the question to ChatGPT and disagreed with some of his/its answers (contract drafting for example).
But I am convinced that it could be interesting to delegate :
- translating text into 2 or more languages
- tracking progress on tasks
- scheduling meetings & coordinating team calendars
- sorting & categorizing information
- drafting & sending routine emails/messages
- creating to do lists
- organizing travel itineraries
- offering feedbacks on essays / exercises
- generating quizzes, flashcards, or study guides
- offering mental health check-ins / or motivational tips
- transcribing audio or video content
- brainstorming ideas for projects.
- generate exams / quizzes based on themes / class notes.
- Summary of class or articles
- explaining juridical concept (with ref to sources Codes, Law, Acts...)

- weather forecast
- sabotaging the LLM -> ask it to give the same answer to all its users -

- update my (ChatGPT) linkedin
- track all the website where I'm mentioned -
- track all the best auctions happening in the world
- make it to create a tailored newsletter send it to me daily (or 2x a day), weekly -..

- data cleaning
- communication tasks.
- work hours @ law firms .
- exhibit organizations
